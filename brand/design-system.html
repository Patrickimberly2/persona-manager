Component patterns

Buttons

States: hover lifts 1px; focus-visible shows cyan ring; active returns to rest with reduced shadow. Disabled reduces opacity to 50% and blocks pointer events.

Form fields

States: default muted border; hover darkens border; focus adds cyan ring and shadow; disabled lowers opacity. For errors, switch border to Amber (#F59E0B) with matching helper text.

Cards

Persona Snapshot

Summaries for galleries, dashboards, and templates. Use 20–24px padding and 12–16px radius.
